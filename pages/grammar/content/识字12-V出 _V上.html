V出 🔎 结果补语

Verb + 出 (chū) : to successfully obtain information / result through the action
💡 核心意思：通过动作“得到/分辨出”一个明确的结果。
🔹 “出” 表示动作有了成果 —— 就像从模糊中“提取”出答案或信息。
🔹 常用于感官(看/听/闻) 和 思维(猜/问/说) 等动词后。
结构英文解释例句
看出to see / tell (by looking)我看出他不开心。
I can tell he is unhappy.
听出to recognize / hear clearly你听出这首歌的名字了吗？
Did you figure out the name of this song?
闻出to smell / detect by smell我闻出了咖啡的味道。
I smelled the coffee.
认出to recognize (someone)十年后我认出了她。
I recognized her after ten years.
猜出to guess correctly他猜出了谜语答案。
He guessed the riddle correctly.
问出to find out (by asking)警察问出了真相。
The police found out the truth.
说出to speak out / utter他终于说出了答案。
He finally said the answer.
📌 注意：看 ≠ 看出；听 ≠ 听出。“看出”强调结果：成功辨认/了解。
🧪 否定式：没 + V + 出 (例：我没听出区别)    可能式：V + 得/不 + 出 (看得出 / 看不出)

V上 🎯 接触 / 达成 / 开始

Verb + 上 (shàng) : to successfully make contact, reach a goal, or start an action
💡 核心意象：动作“接触并附着”到目标上 → 引申为达成目标、进入状态或开始持续。
💡 Core image: The action "touches and attaches" to the target → extended to mean achieving a goal, entering a state, or starting a process.
🔹 四大类（Four main types）：
① 附着 (贴上/穿上) — attach (stick on/put on)
② 达成目标 (考上/爱上/当上) — achieve a goal (get into/fall in love with/become)
③ 开始持续 (说上/吃上) — begin and continue (start talking/start eating)
④ 添加 (加上) — add (add on)
分类结构·例句英文解释
📎 附着 attach 贴tiē上邮票yóupiào / 穿上外套tào / 关上窗户chuānghùstick on / put on / close
🏆 达成目标 achieve a goal考上高中 / 爱上她 / 当上经理get into / fall in love with / become
▶️ 开始并持续 begin and continue说上话 / 用上新手机 / 住zhù上新房fáng子start talking / start using / move into
➕ 添加 add 加jiā上名字 / 写上地址add on / fill in
📌 对比：考上大学 (成功录取) vs 考到600分 (达到分数点)
⚠️ “爱上”是瞬间动词，可说“爱上她三年了”(表示从爱上至今三年)。
🧪 否定：没+ V + 上 (没考上)   可能式：V + 得/不 + 上 (考得上/考不上)
💡 ENGLISH NOTES

📌 Compare: 考上 vs 考到
• 考上 = "get INTO" (a school, a platform)
   → 她考上了大学。 = She got into college.
• 考到 = "reach UP TO" (a number, a point)
   → 她考到了600分。 = She scored up to 600 points.
💬 Think: 上 = onto a surface/platform  |  到 = up to a point/limit

⚠️ 爱上 (fall in love) is a "punctual verb" — it happens in a MOMENT.
• You CANNOT say: ❌ 我爱上她三年。
• You MUST say: ✅ 我爱上她三年了。
   → Meaning: "It has been three years since I fell in love with her."
• Structure: V上 + Object + 了 + Duration = "It has been [time] since the event happened."
   Examples: 他考上大学两年了。 / 她当上经理一年了。

🧪 Negative & Potential Forms
• Negative: 没 + V + 上 = didn't successfully V
   → 他没考上大学。 = He didn't get into college.
• Potential (ability): V + 得/不 + 上 = can / cannot successfully V
   → 他考得上大学。 = He can get into college.
   → 他考不上大学。 = He cannot get into college.

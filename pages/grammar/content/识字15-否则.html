📖 否则 · fǒuzé

otherwise / if not (negative consequence)
🎯 H3 (中高级) · 基于450汉字
1 What does “否则” mean?

否则 = if not · otherwise
It connects two parts:
➤ Part 1: a suggestion, duty, or rule
➤ Part 2: the bad result if you don't follow Part 1.

📍 From your sentence #15:
“收到客人送的礼物以后一般不打开看，否则会让人笑话和被人议论。”
→ Usually don't open the gift in front of the guest. If you open it, people will laugh and talk about you.

🌐 例句 · Bilingual (英/西)

✅ 快点儿，否则要迟到了。
English: Hurry up, otherwise you'll be late.
Español: Date prisa, si no llegarás tarde.
✅ 天气冷了，多穿点儿，否则会生病。
English: It's cold. Wear more clothes, otherwise you'll get sick.
Español: Hace frío. Ponte más ropa, si no te enfermarás.
✅ 复习，否则考试不会好。
English: Review your lessons, otherwise your exam results won't be good.
Español: Repasa las lecciones, si no los resultados del examen no serán buenos.

🌍 STEP 3 例句 · 英/西翻译

📅 早上七点一刻要起床，否则会来不及骑车到教室。
English: You have to get up at 7:15, otherwise you won't have time to bike to the classroom.
Español: Tienes que levantarte a las 7:15, si no, no tendrás tiempo de ir en bicicleta al aula.
🍽️ 问服务员“您贵姓”要客气，否则人家会不高兴。
English: Be polite when asking a server "What's your surname?", otherwise they will be unhappy.
Español: Sé educado al preguntarle a un camarero "¿Cuál es su apellido?", si no, se enojarán.
📚 考试以前要好好复习数理化，否则成绩不会好。
English: You should review math and science well before the exam, otherwise your grades won't be good.
Español: Debes repasar bien matemáticas y ciencias antes del examen, si no, tus calificaciones no serán buenas.
🎁 收到礼物以后一般不打开看，否则会让人笑话。
English: Usually you don't open a gift after receiving it, otherwise people will laugh at you.
Español: Generalmente no se abre un regalo después de recibirlo, si no, la gente se reirá de ti.
🗺️ 去“新世界”商店以前要看导游图，否则会找不到。
English: Check the tourist map before going to the "New World" store, otherwise you won't find it.
Español: Mira el mapa turístico antes de ir a la tienda "Nuevo Mundo", si no, no la encontrarás.
4 Compare: “否则” vs “…的话”
📘 否则 (more formal, written)
快起床，否则会迟到。
🗣️ 不…的话 (more spoken)
快起床，不起的话会迟到。

✅ Sentence #15 uses 否则 because it's about a traditional habit (formal/written style).

5 ⚠️ Common Mistake
❌ Wrong: 我否则不去上课。
(Do NOT put “否则” before the subject “我”).
✅ Correct: 你要去上课，否则老师会不高兴。
(“否则” comes after the suggestion, before the bad result.)

✨ Remember: “否则” must follow a complete clause (order/suggestion) + bad consequence.

8 Summary & Self-Check
  • ✔️ “否则” = otherwise / if not → bad result.
  • ✔️ Structure: Suggestion/Order + 否则 + Bad thing.
  • ✔️ Never put “否则” before the subject (❌ 我否则…).
  • ✔️ Always state the negative consequence after “否则”.
  • ✔️ “否则” is more formal; in spoken Chinese we sometimes use “要不”.
🔁 [Do this] ，否则 [bad result will happen]

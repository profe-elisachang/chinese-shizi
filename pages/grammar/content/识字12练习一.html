疑问代词的非疑问用法 — Interrogative pronouns as indefinites
In Chinese, question words like 谁、什么、哪儿、怎么 can be used not to ask questions, but to mean anyone / anything / anywhere / any way. They are paired with 都 (affirmative) or 也 (negative).
谁都…（everyone） 什么都…（everything） 什么也不…（nothing） 哪儿都…（everywhere） 怎么…都行（any way is fine）
Q 01拖拽排序
Arrange the words: "Every film fan loves this festival."
Click a word to place it · Click a placed word to remove it
Answer
每个电影迷都喜欢这个节日。
每个…都 = "every single …". 都 is used because the sentence is affirmative — it covers all members positively.
Q 02拖拽排序
Arrange the words: "I don't want to eat anything."
Click a word to place it · Click a placed word to remove it
Answer
我什么也不想吃。
什么 + 也 + negative verb = "nothing at all". Use 也 (not 都) in negative sentences.
Q 03拖拽排序
Arrange the words: "No matter how you go, it's fine."
Click a word to place it · Click a placed word to remove it
Answer
你怎么去都行。
怎么 + 都 = "any way / however you do it". 都行 means "it's all fine / acceptable".
Q 04看图造句
Look at the scene and write a sentence using 谁都 or 哪儿都.
🎬🍿😄
节日电影节 · 全城热议
Everyone in the city is talking about the film festival
用"谁都"或"哪儿都"造一个句子：
Reference answers 参考答案
① 谁都知道这个电影节。
② 哪儿都在谈这个节日。
③ 谁都想去看这部电影。
Any sentence with 谁都 or 哪儿都 + verb in an affirmative context is correct. Check: is 都 placed after the pronoun and before the verb?
Q 05看图造句
Look at the scene and write a sentence using 什么也不.
😶‍🌫️🍽️
她没有食欲 · 一口也没吃
She has no appetite — didn't eat a single bite
用"什么也不"或"什么也没"造句：
Reference answers 参考答案
① 她什么也不想吃。
② 她什么也没吃。
③ 她什么瘦肉也不吃。
什么也不 (present/habitual) vs 什么也没 (past — something didn't happen). Both are correct here.
Q 06填空
他是搞美学的，都懂一点儿。
He studies aesthetics — he knows a bit about ___. (Fill with the correct pronoun: who / what / where)
Answer — 什么
他是搞美学的，什么都懂一点儿。
什么都 = "everything / all things". The sentence is affirmative, so 都 is used. (谁都 would mean "everyone knows him a little" — a different meaning.)
Q 07选择题
她高中生活很简单，哪儿不去，只待在学校。
Her high school life is very simple — she doesn't go anywhere, just stays at school.
Answer — 也
她高中生活很简单，哪儿也不去，只待在学校。
The sentence is negative (不去), so use 也. Rule: affirmative → 都 · negative → 也.
Q 08找错题
Which sentence is correct? 哪句话是对的？
Answers — C and D are correct
C ✓ 目前谁都支持他的想法。（affirmative + 都 ✓）
D ✓ 他怎么也学不会简化字。（negative + 也 ✓）
A is wrong: 什么都 + negative = redundant with 不 (should be 什么都不 without the extra 喜欢吃 split, or rewrite as 什么肉都不喜欢吃). B is wrong: 哪儿也 must pair with a negative verb — 去过 is affirmative, so it should be 哪儿都去过了.
结果补语 — Resultative Complements V出、V上、V到、V见
These verbs are placed after the main verb to show the result of the action. Each one carries a distinct meaning.
V 上 ★ Focus
Achieve / Enter / Attach / Fall into
上 signals that the action successfully reached a goal — getting into a school, falling in love, landing a job, or attaching something. The idea is "making contact with" or "getting onto" a higher level.
考上高中 — passed the exam & got in
爱上她 — fell in love with her
贴上名片 — stick the business card on
V 出 ★ Focus
Identify / Perceive / Produce outward
出 signals that something emerges or is extracted from the action — recognising an identity, figuring something out, or producing something. Think: the result "comes out".
认出她 — recognised / identified her
说出答案 — managed to say the answer
看出特点 — noticed the characteristic
V 到 Review
Reach / Obtain / Encounter
到 signals the action reached its target — whether an object, a place, or a time. Very common and versatile.
买到那本书 — managed to buy the book
遇到朋友 — ran into a friend
学到七月 — studied until July
V 见 Review
Sense / Perceive (through senses)
见 emphasises sensory perception — you successfully heard, saw, or smelled something. Often interchangeable with 到 for hearing and seeing.
听见那个节目 — heard the programme
看见那位美学家 — caught sight of the aesthetician
闻见香味 — smelled the aroma
练习 — focus on V上 & V出
Q 09选择题 · V上
他终于___上了北京电台的工作。
He finally landed a job at Beijing Radio Station. — Which verb goes in the blank?
Answer — 找
他终于找上了北京电台的工作。
找上 = found and secured (a job/opportunity). V上 here means successfully making contact with a goal. 考上 would also work if it were an exam context — but "landing a job" uses 找上.
Q 10填空 · V出
你能___出她是哪国人吗？
Are you able to identify which country she is from? — Fill in the verb before 出.
Answer — 说 / 看 / 认
你能说出她是哪国人吗？
你能看出她是哪国人吗？
说出 = manage to state / identify verbally. 看出 = perceive / figure out visually. Both are natural here. 认出 = recognise by sight. All three use 出 because the result is "bringing out" an identity.
Q 11选择 V上 or V出
他一看图片，就认___了那位美学家。
The moment he saw the photo, he recognised the aesthetician.
Answer — 出
他一看图片，就认出了那位美学家。
认出 = to identify / recognise (an identity emerges). 认上 is not standard Chinese. 认到 and 认见 are also not used — 出 is the correct complement for 认.
Q 12拖拽排序 · V上
Arrange: "She fell in love with a handsome and tall American."
Click a word to place it · Click a placed word to remove it
Answer
她爱上了一个高大的美国人。
爱上 = to fall in love with. V上 here signals successfully entering the state of love — "love landed / took hold".
Q 13选择题 · V出
他的主要特点很明显，一眼就能看___。
His main characteristics are very obvious — you can see them at a glance.
Answer — 出 (看见 also accepted)
他的主要特点很明显，一眼就能看出。
（看见 is also natural here.）
看出 = to perceive / figure out (something is revealed). 看见 = to catch sight of (sensory). Both work — but 出 is preferred when the emphasis is on perceiving / noticing a characteristic rather than simply seeing something.
能愿动词 能、想、要、可以 — Quick Review
You already know these well! Quick recap: modal verbs go before the main verb and are negated with 不.
能 ability/possibility · 想 wish/intention · 要 want/will · 可以 permission/possibility
不能 not able 不想 don't want to 不要 won't / don't 不可以 not allowed
Q 14填空
他目前___当电视节目主持人。（expressing a wish）
He currently ___ to become a TV host. Use the most natural modal verb.
Answer — 想 (or 要)
他目前想当电视节目主持人。
想 = softer personal wish/hope. 要 = stronger intention/will. The dialogue uses 想 — it sounds more natural here since it's a dream, not a firm plan.
Q 15选择题
你___说出她是哪国人吗？（asking about ability）
___ you identify which country she is from? Choose the correct modal.
Answer — 能
你能说出她是哪国人吗？
能 asks about capability/skill. 可以 asks about permission — not the right fit here. 想/要 express intention, not ability.
Q 16找错题
Which sentence has an error? 哪句话有错？
Answer — B
正确说法：我不要买这本简单的书。
In B, 要不 is wrong word order — 不 must come directly before the modal: 不要. Note: 要不 is a real phrase meaning "otherwise / or else", which completely changes the meaning.

疑问代词的非疑问用法 — Interrogative pronouns as indefinites
谁 / 什么 / 哪儿 / 怎么 ＋ 都 / 也
Used not to ask questions, but to mean "anyone / anything / anywhere / any way" — usually followed by 都 (positive) or 也 (negative).
谁都… 什么也不… 哪儿都… 怎么…都行

结果补语 出、上、到、见 — Resultative complements
动词 ＋ 出 / 上 / 到 / 见
出 (identify/perceive) · 上 (achieve/enter) · 到 (reach/obtain) · 见 (sense/encounter) — placed after the main verb to show the result of the action.
认出 考上 买到 遇到 听见 / 听到 爱上
Q 04选择题 Multiple choice
他一眼就认了那个美食家。
He recognised the gourmet at a glance.
Answer 答案 — 出
他一眼就认出了那个美食家。
认出 = to identify / recognise. 出 signals that something is "brought out" — an identity is revealed or perceived successfully.
Q 05选择题 Multiple choice
他考了高中，现在是高中生了。
He passed the entrance exam and is now a senior high school student.
Answer 答案 — 上
他考上了高中，现在是高中生了。
考上 = to pass an exam and gain entry. 上 indicates successful achievement of a goal or entering a new state/place.
Q 06选择题 Multiple choice
我在图书馆遇了一个美学家。
I ran into an aesthetician at the library.
Answer 答案 — 到
我在图书馆遇到了一个美学家。
遇到 = to run into / encounter. 到 indicates the action reached its object. 见 also works (遇见) — both are accepted in modern Chinese.
Q 07选择题 Multiple choice
他在电台里听了那个节目。
He heard that programme on the radio station.
Answer 答案 — 到 or 见
他在电台里听到了那个节目。/ 他在电台里听见了那个节目。
Both 听到 and 听见 mean "to hear (successfully)". 见 emphasises sensory perception; 到 emphasises that the listening reached its result. Both are correct.

能愿动词 能、想、要、可以 — Modal verbs
能 / 想 / 要 / 可以 ＋ 动词（negated with 不）
能 (ability/possibility) · 想 (wish/intention) · 要 (want/will) · 可以 (permission/possibility). Always placed before the main verb. Negated by adding 不 before the modal.
能够说出 不想吃 要买 可以来 不可以去
Q 08填空 Fill in the blank
他目前当电视节目主持人。
He currently wants to become a TV host. (expressing a personal wish)
Answer 答案 — 想 (or 要)
他目前想当电视节目主持人。
想 expresses a personal wish or hope — softer than 要. 要 also works but sounds more decisive/determined. The dialogue uses 想, so that's the primary answer.
Q 09填空 Fill in the blank
你说出她是哪国人吗？
Are you able to identify which country she is from? (asking about ability)
Answer 答案 — 能 (or 能够)
你能说出她是哪国人吗？
能 and 能够 both express capability/ability. The dialogue uses 能够 — both are accepted here. 可以 leans toward permission, so it's a weaker choice.
Q 10找错题 Spot the error
下面哪句话有语法错误？
Which sentence contains a grammar error?
Answer 答案 — B
正确说法：我不要买这本简单的书。
In sentence B, 要不 is wrong word order. The negative 不 must come directly before the modal: 不要. Note: 要不 is a real phrase but means "otherwise / or else" — a completely different meaning.

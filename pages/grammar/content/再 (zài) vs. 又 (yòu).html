再 (zài) vs. 又 (yòu)

Understanding the Difference Between "Again" in Chinese

The Core Rule: Time

Word Time & Function Feeling
再 (zài) Future / Not Yet Happened
Used for an action you will repeat or add later.		 Subjective: Shows your plan, wish, request, or suggestion.
又 (yòu) Past / Already Happened
Used for an action that has already been repeated.		 Objective: Just states a fact that happened.

1. 再 (zài) - For the Future

Use 再 when you are talking about doing something again in the future.

  • Common Patterns:
    • 想再 + Verb (want to do again)
    • 要再 + Verb (will do again)
    • 可以再 + Verb (can do again)
    • 请再 + Verb (please do again)
    • 下次再 + Verb (next time do again)
  • Examples:
    • 今天我很忙，明天再见。
      (Jīntiān wǒ hěn máng, míngtiān zài jiàn.)
      I'm busy today, see you again tomorrow.
    • 这杯咖啡很好，我要再买一杯。
      (Zhè bēi kāfēi hěn hǎo, wǒ yào zài mǎi yì bēi.)
      This coffee is great, I will buy another cup.
    • 你说得太快了，请再说一次。
      (Nǐ shuō de tài kuài le, qǐng zài shuō yī cì.)
      You spoke too fast, please say it again.

2. 又 (yòu) - For the Past

Use 又 when you are talking about something that happened again in the past. It is often used with 了 (le).

  • Common Patterns:
    • 又 + Verb + 了 (did something again)
    • 昨天又...了 (yesterday did... again)
    • 他又来了。 (Tā yòu lái le.) - He came again.
  • Examples:
    • 他昨天迟到了，今天又迟到了。
      (Tā zuótiān chídào le, jīntiān yòu chídào le.)
      He was late yesterday, and he was late again today.
    • 我很饱，可是她又给了我一个苹果。
      (Wǒ hěn bǎo, kěshì tā yòu gěi le wǒ yí gè píngguǒ.)
      I was full, but she gave me an apple again.
    • 天气预报说，明天又下雨。
      (Tiānqì yùbào shuō, míngtiān yòu xià yǔ.)
      Special Case: 又 for Future - This is only used for predicting a repeated situation, often with a feeling of complaint or impatience. (e.g., "Oh no, it's going to rain again tomorrow.")

Quick Test: Can You Feel the Difference?

Compare these two sentences:

  1. 明天我想再去那家餐厅。
    (Míngtiān wǒ xiǎng zài qù nà jiā cāntīng.)
    Time: Future (tomorrow).
    Feeling: My plan or wish.
    Translation: I want to go to that restaurant again tomorrow.
  2. 昨天我又去了那家餐厅。
    (Zuótiān wǒ yòu qù le nà jiā cāntīng.)
    Time: Past (yesterday).
    Feeling: Just a fact.
    Translation: I went to that restaurant again yesterday.

Memory Tip

  • 再 (zài) sounds like "在" (zài - at/in), which can point to a time in the future.
  • 又 (yòu) looks like a hand doing an action, and that action has already left a mark.

Practice: Fill in the blanks with 再 or 又

Try to complete these sentences. Remember: Future = 再, Past = 又.

1 这本书我看完了，明天我想（   ）看一遍。

(I finished this book, I want to read it _____ tomorrow.)

正确答案
再 - This is about a future action (tomorrow), so we use 再.
2 他昨天迟到了，今天（   ）迟到了。

(He was late yesterday, and he was late _____ today.)

正确答案
又 - This describes something that already happened (he was late again today), so we use 又.
3 别着急，我们可以（   ）试一次。

(Don't worry, we can try _____.)

正确答案
再 - This is about a future possibility (we can try again), so we use 再.
4 你（   ）把钥匙忘在家里了？下次要小心。

(You forgot your keys at home _____? Be careful next time.)

正确答案
又 - This describes something that already happened (you forgot again), so we use 又.
5 音乐很好听，请（   ）放一次。

(The music is nice, please play it _____.)

正确答案
再 - This is a request for a future action (please play it again), so we use 再.
6 我去年去了北京，今年我（   ）去了。

(I went to Beijing last year, and I went _____ this year.)

正确答案
又 - This describes something that already happened (I went again this year), so we use 又.

练习指引

Instructions:

请将原句（描述计划或建议），参照例句，改写成句子（描述已发生的事实）。注意动词和时间词的变化，并正确使用 再 或 又。

Please rewrite the original sentence (describing plans or suggestions) into a sentence (describing facts that have already happened). Pay attention to changes in verbs and time words, and use 再 or 又 correctly.

此设计能让你清晰对比「再」（用于主观意愿、未来）与「又」（用于客观描述、过去）的语境差异，强化语感与应用能力。

This design helps you clearly compare the contextual differences between "再" (used for subjective intentions, future) and "又" (used for objective descriptions, past), strengthening your language sense and application ability.

一 练习一
原句（未发生）：
菜单上的甜点看起来很美味，如果我们还吃得下，饭后再点一份巧克力蛋糕吧。
The desserts on the menu look delicious. If we still have room, let's order another chocolate cake after the meal.
改写（已发生）：
请在此处写出你的改写句子...
Please write your rewritten sentence here...
正确答案
菜单上的甜点看起来很美味，因为我们还吃得下，饭后又点了一份巧克力蛋糕。
The desserts on the menu looked delicious. Since we still had room, we ordered another chocolate cake after the meal.

解析：原句是未来的计划（"再点"），改写后描述已发生的事实（"又点了"）。注意时间词从"如果"变为"因为"，动词从"点"变为"点了"。

Explanation: The original sentence is a future plan ("再点"), while the rewritten sentence describes a fact that has already happened ("又点了"). Note the time word changes from "如果" (if) to "因为" (because), and the verb changes from "点" to "点了".

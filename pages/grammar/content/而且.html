而且 HSK 2 · 3

连词 Conjunction · "and also / moreover"
📖 MEANING simple & clear
而且 = and also / plus / moreover
ér qiě
✅ Use it to ADD extra information.
➡️ The second part is often STRONGER or more important.
💡 Quick rule: Put a comma before 而且. Connect adjectives, verb phrases, or whole sentences.
✨ EXAMPLES 中文 + English translation
① 这件衣服漂亮，而且便宜。
📌 This piece of clothing is beautiful, and also cheap.
🔹 Adj + 而且 + Adj
② 他会说中文，而且说得很好。
📌 He can speak Chinese, and moreover speaks it very well.
🔹 Verb phrase + 而且 + Verb phrase
③ 这家餐厅味道好，而且价格不贵。
📌 This restaurant tastes good, and also the prices are not expensive.
🔹 Sentence + 而且 + Sentence
④ 他学习很努力，而且很聪明。
📌 He studies very hard, and also is very smart.
⑤ 这个手机很新，而且电池很好。
📌 This phone is very new, plus the battery is great.
⚡ Compare: 和 (hé) vs 而且 (ér qiě)
和 hé
→ only for NOUNS
e.g. 苹果和香蕉
而且 ér qiě
→ for adjectives / verbs / sentences
e.g. 便宜，而且好看
⚠️ Common mistake: Don't use 和 with adjectives or sentences!
❌ 他漂亮和努力    ✅ 他漂亮，而且努力
